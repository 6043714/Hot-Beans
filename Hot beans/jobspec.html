Specification and Requirements

Here You Will Find A list Of The Requirements To Work At Hot Beans:

You Must Be able To:

-Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: Proficiency in these fundamental web development languages is essential for starting as a trainee web developer at Hot Beans.
-Familiarity with Web Development Frameworks: Basic understanding of popular web development frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js is preferred.
-Understanding of Responsive Design: A grasp of responsive design principles to ensure websites adapt seamlessly across different devices and screen sizes.
-Problem-Solving Skills: Demonstrated ability to identify and solve coding challenges efficiently and effectively.
-Attention to Detail: Strong focus on accuracy and precision when coding, to deliver high-quality websites.
-Continuous Learning Attitude: Willingness to learn and stay updated with the latest web technologies and trends in the industry.
-Team Player: Ability to collaborate and communicate effectively with team members, as web development often involves working in teams.
-Portfolio or Projects: Providing a portfolio or examples of previous web development projects, even if they are personal or educational, can showcase practical skills and dedication.
-Educational Background: While not mandatory, a degree or certification in computer science or a related field is a plus.
-Passion for Web Development: Demonstrating a genuine interest and passion for web development, with a desire to grow and expand skills in the field.
